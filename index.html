Classic Game Review: GTA:San Andreas

March 12, 2024

Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas is an iconic open-world game that offers unparalleled freedom and a gripping story. Rockstar made an absolute gem of a game with this one. The depth of its characters, the immersive 90s atmosphere, and the sheer scope of exploration make it a standout title in the GTA series and a timeless classic.
